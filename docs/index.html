🐾 PetApp3

Next‑generation AI Virtual Pet Application

Overview

PetApp3 is an AI‑powered virtual pet application that generates realistic images and short videos of your own pets—dogs, cats, and rabbits —based on 15 emotional and behavioral states.

This version introduces a major upgrade: English prompt generation with first‑person perspective, powered by the new PromptGenerator_en module.

Key Features

  • 15 pet states (n1–n3, p1–p12)
  • English prompt generation with first‑person perspective
  • Species‑specific Joy behavior (dog / cat / rabbit)
  • Affection (p12) reacts to your custom affection word
  • Supports external AI tools (Copilot, Gemini, Pika, Runway)

How It Works

  1. Register your pet’s species, appearance, and affection word.
  2. Select a state (n1–n3, p1–p12).
  3. PetApp3 generates an English prompt tailored to your pet.
  4. Use the prompt in your preferred AI tool to generate images or videos.
  5. Assign the generated media to each state.
  6. Watch your pet react in real time in the PlayWindow.
